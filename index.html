Art Collage Generator

Welcome to the Art Collage Generator! This generator pulls pictures from the RIJKS Museum in Amsterdam using their RIJKS api. To generate a collage you will select an art style and a color. The generated collage will be a combination of RIJKS Museum pieces and color blocks! Have fun!

Prints

Pick from a large selection of prints from the RIJKS museum. These prints vary in style, from classic 17th century to more modern 21st century pieces. The styles much more variable if you select prints, something you might want to keep in mind when you're choosing a medium.

Paintings

Pick from a large selection of painted images from the RIJKS museum collection. Many of these paintings have a more medeival vibe to them. While these styles do not vary greatly, the similarities can create a cohesive and aesthetic collage. This might be something to consider when choosing a medium.
